About

My name is Catherine Davis and I'm a soon to be graduate from Bloc.io in their web development track. I'm passionate about both functional and beautiful websites that draw people in and get their message heard. Got bugs? I'm a problem-solving whiz who loves to tackle just about anything you could throw at me. I've worked in many industries from customer-service to helping people with developmental disabilites.
